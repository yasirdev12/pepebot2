Our Purpose

PepeNetwork is a hyper deflationary token built on the Binance Smart Chain Network. Its inception was driven by the mission to provide investors with an improved and more secure experience when engaging in the purchase, sale, and trading of various cryptocurrencies within the dynamic crypto space.

Anti-dump System

To ensure that we don’t have people selling huge amounts of tokens at once, which could have a detrimental impact on the project, we have decided to implement an anti-dump system that only allows people to sell 0.15% of total supply every 24 hours the first few weeks. We chose to have this instead of having a vesting period for the private and presale buyers.
